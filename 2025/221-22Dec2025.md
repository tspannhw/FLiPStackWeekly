*The core of the Data Cloud is evolving rapidly with new storage and compute options.*

### **Postgres on Snowflake (Public Preview)**

Snowflake has officially launched the public preview of **Snowflake for Postgres**. This allows you to leverage the familiar Postgres interface while benefiting from Snowflake's scale.

* [Engineering Blog: Postgres Public Preview](https://www.snowflake.com/en/engineering-blog/postgres-public-preview/)  
* [Developer Guide: Getting Started with Snowflake Postgres](https://www.snowflake.com/en/developers/guides/getting-started-with-snowflake-postgres/)

### **Iceberg & Open Standards**

* **Private Rest Catalogs**: New documentation on configuring [Private REST Catalog integrations for Iceberg Tables](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/tables-iceberg-configure-catalog-integration-rest-private).  
* **Polaris Tools**: Check out the new [Console for Apache Polaris](https://github.com/apache/polaris-tools/tree/main/console) to manage your open lakehouse.

### **Snowsight & Workspaces**

Snowflake Notebooks have officially moved into [Workspaces](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/ui-snowsight/notebooks-in-workspaces/notebooks-in-workspaces-overview), providing a more collaborative and structured environment for data science and engineering teams.
